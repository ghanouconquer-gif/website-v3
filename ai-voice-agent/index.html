Frequently Asked Questions

Can the AI Voice Agent differentiate between emergency plumbing/HVAC and routine maintenance?

Yes. The AI runs on customized logic trees that instantly categorize calls based on urgency. If a caller says 'my pipe burst,' the AI triggers the emergency dispatch protocol and pages the on-call technician. If they say 'I need my seasonal tune-up,' it offers standard calendar slots.

What happens if a customer asks a complex technical question the AI doesn't know?

The AI is explicitly trained with safety rails. If it encounters a diagnostic question outside its training matrix (e.g., specific voltage requirements for a rare capacitor), it will smoothly admit it cannot diagnose over the phone and immediately pivot to booking a senior technician for an in-person assessment, or route the call directly to a human manager if specifically requested.

What exactly is an AI voice agent, and how is it different from a traditional answering service?

An AI answering system is software that handles business calls without a human on the line — it greets callers, understands requests, gathers details, and either resolves the issue or routes the call with full context. Unlike traditional IVR systems that force callers through rigid number-press menus, modern AI agents use natural language processing so customers speak naturally. They can also capture leads and schedule appointments 24/7, eliminating the revenue lost to voicemail and hold abandonment.

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI, and will they get frustrated?

Modern AI voice systems use advanced voice synthesis to produce realistic pauses and intonations. In many cases, callers do not realize they are speaking to an AI because of the software's emotional expression and empathetic responses. For calls that escalate beyond the AI's scope — complex complaints or requests that specifically ask for a human — the UNBACKED agent is configured with hard safety rails that immediately transfer the call to your team with full context intact.
