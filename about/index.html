The Origin Protocol

Abdelghani Mellal founded UNBACKED after auditing a 34-year HVAC company in Tampa — NATE certified, 25,000 service calls, 4.9-star average across 1,000+ reviews — and discovering it scored 29 out of 90 on AI visibility. The company had done everything right for three decades. Its reputation was real. Its customers were loyal. And it was completely invisible to the AI systems a growing share of those customers were using to find contractors. Meanwhile a competitor with 2 years of history and a fraction of the reputation was the one getting recommended. UNBACKED was built to close that gap—especially given that businesses with complete semantic profiles see a 4.2x increase in AI citations—to take the home service businesses that earned their market over years of real work and make sure the infrastructure matches the reputation. So when a homeowner asks an AI for the best HVAC company in their city, the answer is the business that deserves to be there.

The UNBACKED philosophy is strict:

We do not sell "leads." We construct a closed-loop system that captures intent via Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), immediately recovers missed calls via AI Voice Agents, and traps backend revenue via Ops Intelligence. The 9-pillar framework draws on GEO research published at KDD 2024 by Aggarwal et al. from Princeton University and Georgia Tech. It is an infrastructure upgrade, not a marketing campaign.
